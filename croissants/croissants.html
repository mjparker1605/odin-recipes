Croissants

Description

These flaky, laminated pastries from France will be sure to please. Lamination is the process of laying butter in between the dough and created multiple dough-butter layers that combines create that beautiful honeycomb structure that croissants are known for.

Ingredients

For Dough

  • 605 grams flour
  • 66 grams sugar
  • 12 grams salt
  • 7 grams active dry yeast
  • 214 grams water(room temp)
  • 120 grams whole milk
  • 57 grams unsalted butter(chilled)

For Butter block and assmbly

  • 340 grams european butter
  • 1 Large egg
  • 1 tbsp heavy cream

Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

An american classic. Not much more to say.

Ingredients

  • Bread (white or wheat)
  • Jelly (grape,strawberry,or raspberry)
  • Peanut Butter (crunchy or smooth

Steps

  1. Lay out two slices of bread out on a flat surface
  2. On one side of the bread spread your peanut butter.
  3. On the other slice of bread, spread the jelly in a ration approx. 2-1 Peanut Butter to Jelly(THIS IS A VERY IMPORTANT STEP)
  4. Put the bread together with the jelly and peanutbutter sides facing eachother
  5. Cut the bread in half
